Billiards: The VPython project 

Brief game description: 
Our goal was to create a Vpython game based on pool. The game wil start with a 
normal pool setup. Then players will take turns hitting the que ball, trying to 
get either stripes or solids in.

Controls:
press SPACE to shoot
press R to rescue cue ball
RIGHT_CLICK to set angle of the shot
Use the bar to the right to control strength of shot

How to win:
The text box will display the score of the players 
First player to reach seven balls wins
